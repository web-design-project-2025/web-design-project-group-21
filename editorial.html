🏛️ A Cultural Weekend in Gothenburg

Published May 10, 2025

Dive into Gothenburg’s vibrant culture with our weekend guide. Start your day at the Göteborgs Konstmuseum, home to Nordic masters and contemporary gems. Then, stroll down Haga for a fika and some vintage shopping.

Don’t miss the Röda Sten art center by the water – industrial vibes meet modern art. In the evening, check out a performance at the Gothenburg Opera or grab dinner in the creative Magasinsgatan area.
